What can I say about the mighty Skyr Bowl?

One of the most energising snacks a boy could eat.

Turns you into an absolute gym BEAST. You will hit your PR after your consume this bad boy.

Ingredients

  • Skyr
  • Blueberries
  • Granola
  • Dark chocolate
  • Medjool dates
  • Maple syrup

Steps

  1. Put the skyr in a bowl.
  2. Put the granola in the skyr.
  3. Put the dark chocolate in the granola skyr.
  4. Put the blueberries in the dark chocolate granola skyr.
  5. Put a medjool date in the blueberries dark chocolate granola skyr.
  6. Pour the maple syrup over the medjool date blueberry chocolate granola skyr.
  7. Eat every day for the rest of your life.
