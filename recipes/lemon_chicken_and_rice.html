Lemon Chicken & Rice

Click here to return home.

This is a classic, delicious weekday dish that I like to whip up in a pinch.

The dish hits all the staples of a "weekday classic". It takes only 10 minutes to prep, the cooking is easy and then it's in the oven for 35 minutes to work it's magic.

It's got your protein and carbs, natural fats and some nutrition with the addition of the mighty spinach! Feel free to add extra veggies if you want it to be more hearty.

Ingredients

  • Skin on, bone-in chicken thighs
  • Lemon
  • Mixed green herbs
  • Rice
  • Spinach
  • Onion
  • Chicken stock

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken thighs overnight in a homemade lemon and herb marinade.
  2. Pre-heat oven at 180c.
  3. Cook the chicken thighs, starting skin side down, for 5 minutes each side.
  4. Remove the browned chicken and sizzle diced onion in the remaining oil.
  5. Once soft, add garlic, extra green herbs and chopped spinach. Cook for 1 minute.
  6. Add rice and stir. Add chicken stock and stir.
  7. Put the pot with the lid on into the oven for 35 minutes.
