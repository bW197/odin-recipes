Blueberry Pancakes

Click here to return home.

One of my personal favourites... blueberry pancakes!

Do they need much of an introduction? I don't think so... let's get straight into the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Blueberries
  • Maple Syrup

Steps

  1. Whisk together the wet ingredients.
  2. Add the dry ingredients and mix well.
  3. Heat up a pan to a medium-high, then turn down to a medium.
  4. Add a knob of butter.
  5. Add the mix and flip after risen and golden-brown.
